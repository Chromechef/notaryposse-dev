Certified Apostille Service

The Knowledge and ability to quickly get your documents Apostilled or legalized/Authenticated for any foreign country.

An Apostille is a certification of signatures on a document that is going to be used in a foreign country that has joined or contracted with the October 5th, 1961 Hague Convention. If the Country of destination does not belong to the Hague Convention they are known as Non Hague Countries and the process is much more involved and their fee's change drastically.

You know the old adage you don't know what you don't know. Well trying to complete the documents for any Authentication or Apostille is confusing and takes so much time. Maybe you are adopting a child, having a destination wedding, going to study or work in a foreign country. One misstep and you have to start all over and you do not get your money back. Save yourself the frustration, time, hassle and confusion Let us cut through all the red tape for you!
